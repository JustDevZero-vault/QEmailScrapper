Get started with QEmailScrapper

QEmailScrapper is a small scrapper developed using the Qt framework. You can use it to scrap your text files such as notes or documents searching email addresses.

IMAGEN

Once qemailscrapper launches, you can start opening a file. To scrap the file, just click Scrap

To learn about additional qemailscrapper features and to get assistance with other tasks, explore the other portions of help
